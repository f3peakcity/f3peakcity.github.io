## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

**Q: Where can I get bags?**  
A: Contact Nantan or the Challenge Crew listed below. Bags are distributed at AOs throughout the week.

**Q: Can I drop off bags if I'm not in F3?**  
A: Absolutely! Anyone in the Triangle community can participate. Drop bags at any listed AO during workout times (typically early morning).

**Q: What if I can't afford all 9 items?**  
A: Any items help! Fill what you can. Every contribution matters to families in need.

**Q: How long does the challenge run?**  
A: This is a sustained, multi-month effort. We'll continue multiple waves as long as the need exists in our community.

**Q: Can my workplace or organization participate?**  
A: Yes! Contact the Challenge Crew about organizing a workplace drive. We can provide multiple bags for corporate or group participation.
